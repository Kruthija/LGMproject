A SMALL TALK ABOUT HOTELS:

hotel

A hotel is an establishment that provides paid lodging on a short-term basis. Facilities provided inside hotel room may range from a modest-quality mattress in a small room to large suites with bigger, higher-quality beds,a dresser,a refrigirator and other kitchen facilites,upholstered chairs,a flat-screentelevision and en-suite bathrooms.

Different types of hotels:
  • Budget and Value Hotels
  • Mid-Range Hotels and Business Hotels
  • Family Hotels
  • Luxury and High-End Hotels
